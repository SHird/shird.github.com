Welcome to my GitHub Page. I currently have two projects here (click their icons to go to their Download pages): 

PRGmatic reformats next-generation sequencing into diploid loci. It is described fully in Hird, Brumfield and Carstens (2011).  
lociNGS displays large multi-locus datasets and reformats data. I worked on this project for Google Summer of Code 2011 with NESCent.
